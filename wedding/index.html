Amber & Chris Wedding Jan 12th 2025

Walled City Brewery, Derry

2:30 PM

Dress Code

Smart – So suits, dresses etc but don't worry about it too much – we just want you to feel comfortable! Ideally wear something you already own to help save the planet, or find an absolute bargain on good ol' Vinted and leave you more money to spend on delicious, delicious beer.

Order of Ceremony

The ceremony starts in the Walled City Brewery at 2:30 PM – We would invite you to arrive and be seated for no later than 2:15 PM. Following the ceremony there'll be a drinks and canapé reception. Dinner and speeches begin around 5 PM. There will be an evening buffet, before the day wraps up around 12 AM. The entire event takes place on site at the Walled City Brewery.

Where to Stay

The Ebrington Hotel is right next door to the venue.

There is also a Premier Inn a short taxi-journey away

Where to Eat

If you're staying down either-side of the wedding and looking for somewhere to eat Derry has some amazing restaurants – We'd really recommend:
Coupe By The Dirty Souls, an excellent BBQ spot.
Pyke 'N' Pommes , one of our all-time favourites - street food served inside an old double-decker bus.
El Tapas Grá - Spainish Tapas with an Irish twist.
And of course, the Walled City Brewery itself is a delightful spot to eat and drink, if you just can't get enough of it!

Location

The Walled City Brewery is located at the heart of Ebrington Square. The railway station is a short 10 minute walk away. Ebrington Square has a large free carpark, but as far as we can tell it doesn't allow for overnight parking. The Ebrington hotel has a carpark, which costs £6 a night.
